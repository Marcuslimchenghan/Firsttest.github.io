Diploma in Business

This diploma program is designed to produce skilled and knowledgeable individuals in the field of business,
meeting current industry needs. Graduates will be able to solve business problems in creative, ethical, and innovative ways,
show entrepreneurial abilities, and continue learning for future career growth.
It is also one of the few diplomas accepted for entry into the second year of a University of London degree.

Course Highlight

By the end of the program, students will be able to:
  1. Understand and use math and computer science to solve complex IT problems.
  2. Use a structured approach to solve problems with various programming methods and software tools.
  3. Show practical skills in developing, testing, and maintaining software systems.
  4. Work well in a team and use good communication to support teamwork.
  5. Communicate clearly with others through speaking and writing.
  6. Use IT tools to support independent and lifelong learning, especially for finding and using information.
  7. Identify and solve IT problems using basic math concepts and logical thinking.
  8. Follow ethical guidelines and take responsibility for professional behavior.
